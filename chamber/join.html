NP Membership

Features:

Cost: Free for non-profit organizations.
Benefits:
  • Access to local networking events.
  • Basic listing in the business directory.
  • Participation in community-focused initiatives.
  • Invitations to quarterly non-profit roundtables.

Bronze Membership

Features:

Cost: Affordable, entry-level membership.
Benefits:
  • Access to all chamber events at a discounted rate.
  • Standard listing in the business directory with a link to the organization's website.
  • Subscription to the chamber's newsletter for updates and insights.
  • Eligibility to participate in training and workshops.
  • Recognition on the chamber's social media.

Silver Membership

Features:

Cost: Mid-tier membership with added perks.
Benefits:
All Bronze Membership benefits, plus:
  • Enhanced listing in the business directory with a logo and short description.
  • Priority seating and registration for chamber events.
  • Eligibility for spotlight promotions in newsletters or social media.
  • Invitation to member-only networking dinners.
  • Access to mentorship programs with established business leaders.

Gold Membership

Features:

Cost: Premium membership for maximum exposure and benefits.
Benefits:
All Silver Membership benefits, plus:
  • Featured listing in the business directory with premium placement.
  • Free access to all chamber events, including exclusive VIP events.
  • Personalized promotional campaigns through chamber channels.
  • Opportunity to host chamber events or webinars.
  • One-on-one business consultation with chamber advisors.
  • Priority sponsorship opportunities for chamber activities.
  • Annual award consideration as a Gold Member.
